Red Curry


Description

Ingredients

  • Thai Red Curry Paste: 5-6 tbsp
  • Vegetable oil: 3 tbsp
  • Chicken broth: 1 cup
  • Coconut milk: 14 oz
  • Kaffir Lime: 6 leaves
  • Sugar: 1 tbsp
  • Fish sauce: 2 tsp
  • Chicken thighs: 12 oz
  • Pumpkin squash: 5 oz
  • Green beans: 4 oz
  • Thai basil: 12 leaves

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large, heavy based skillet over medium high heat.
  2. Add curry paste and cook for about 2 minutes to "dry it out".
  3. Add coconut milk, lime leaves, sugar, and fish sauce. Stir, then add chicken.
  4. Spread chicken out, bring to simmer, then turn heat down to medium. Simmer for 8-10 minutes.
  5. Taste test the product. Add fish sauce to salten, and sugar to sweeten.
  6. Add pumpkin and beans, then stir. Cook for 3 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat and stir in the Thai basil leaves.
  8. Serve over jasmine rice, with fresh red chili slice and fresh cilantro leaves for some extra pizazz.
